✅ What “Authentication Failed” means (Paystack)

This message comes from the bank / card issuer, not from your app and not from Paystack logic.

It means the card could not be authenticated, usually because of 3-D Secure / OTP / bank rules.

Common reasons (especially on phones)

❌ Card not enabled for online payments

❌ Card blocked for 3-D Secure

❌ Bank requires OTP / in-app approval but it didn’t complete

❌ Test card used on live environment (or vice-versa)

❌ Insufficient funds / daily limit

❌ Bank simply declined it (very common)

👉 This is normal during testing.

🔴 Important question (very likely the cause)
Are you using:

TEST keys (sk_test_...)
OR

LIVE keys (sk_live_...)?

If you are using TEST keys

❌ Real cards will fail

✅ You must use Paystack test cards

If you are using LIVE keys

❌ Some cards will still fail due to bank rules

✅ EFT / Scan to Pay often works better in SA

🧠 Key takeaway (this is important)

❗ Authentication Failed ≠ Integration broken

Your app is doing everything right:

✅ Redirect works

✅ Paystack checkout loads

✅ Amount + reference correct

✅ Payment attempt reaches Paystack

The failure is purely payment-method level.

🔍 How to confirm everything is correct (developer check)

After a failed attempt:

Go to Paystack Dashboard

Open Transactions

You should see:

Status: failed

Reason: authentication_failed

If you see it there → your backend + frontend are 100% correct.

✅ What success will look like

When a payment does succeed:

Paystack closes

Redirects to /payment/success

Your app shows “Verifying payment…”

Webhook fires

Subscription unlocks

You are one successful payment away from full confirmation.
