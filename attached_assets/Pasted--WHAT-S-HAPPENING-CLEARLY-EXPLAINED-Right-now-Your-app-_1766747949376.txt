🧠 PAYSTACK FLOW (THE CORRECT WAY)

Paystack works like this:

🔁 Two separate channels
Channel	Purpose
Redirect (callback_url)	UX only (send user back to app)
Webhook	Source of truth (unlock subscription)

You must not unlock features based on redirect alone.
