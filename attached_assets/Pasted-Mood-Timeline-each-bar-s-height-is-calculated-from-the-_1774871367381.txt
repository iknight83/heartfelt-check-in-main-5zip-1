Mood Timeline — each bar's height is calculated from the actual moodLevel (1–5), coloured red/orange/purple/emerald/green per level. Days with multiple entries show the average. Empty days show a dim placeholder bar. Locked under 3 entries.
Emotional Breakdown — always renders all 5 rows even when count is 0. Percentage is Math.round(count / total * 100). Row colour is dim when 0%, vivid when active. Shows "Based on N entries this month" at the bottom.
Reflective Card — this was the most broken. It was using hardcoded copy ("This month had some challenging moments"). Now it reads your actual entries to find the dominant mood level, calculates the percentage, pulls your real descriptor chips (like "Steady", "Calm"), and if you wrote a note it shows a truncated quote. The title and body text are generated from your real data — 5 different tones depending on dominant mood.
Emotional Consistency — locked until 7 entries (you have 4, so it correctly shows 4/7). The waveform bars now reflect actual daily mood averages — bars are not uniform height anymore, they scale with real data. Score label ("Very stable" / "Fairly steady" / "Some variation" / "High variability") shows once unlocked.
Influence Tracking — the week strip dots only appear on days with actual entries. The sentiment labels ("Often draining" etc.) only appear after a factor has been logged 3+ times with mood data on those same days. Shows "X/3 uses needed to show impact" while building toward that threshold.
What Influenced Your Mood — shows a delta bar per trigger, centred at "Okay" (level 3) as baseline. Positive delta pulls right in green, negative pulls left in red. Shows average mood score and a low-confidence warning if under 5 uses.
Patterns You Might Miss — time-of-day breakdown (Morning/Afternoon/Evening/Night) and weekday bars. Best time is highlighted in blue, best weekday in green, worst weekday in red. Each bucket needs 3 entries minimum to show a value.
Deeper Insights — 4 compact feature cards instead of a blurred wall. Each card shows a real progress bar: entries / required. They light up and unlock individually as you hit each threshold.
